SERVICES

SENDIT offers access to different types of courier services
Do you have a parcel, sports equipment or pallet to ship
and do not know which type of courier service you need?
SENDIT can provide you with the access to
different types of courier services. From standard
shipping of packages and suitcases to freight transport.
The selection of the right type of courier service
depends on what your shipping needs are, how quickly you
would like your shipment to be collected, and of course
your budget.

Standard courier services
Standard shipping is the most common and cheapest courier service used for shipping small to medium size packages

International courier services
Whenever a shipment is sent from one country to another, we talk about international courier services. For these type of courier services, the prices vary depending on the chosen route
